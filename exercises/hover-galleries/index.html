Shrek (2001)

Shrek is about a grumpy ogre who wants his swamp back. To do so, he agrees to rescue Princess Fiona for Lord Farquaad. Along the way, Shrek falls for Fiona, who hides a secret—she turns into an ogre at night. In the end, Fiona chooses Shrek, and they live happily ever after.

Main Characters

  • Donkey: A talkative, loyal friend who sticks by Shrek.
  • Princess Fiona: A strong, independent princess with a secret curse.
  • Lord Farquaad: A power-hungry ruler who wants to marry Fiona to become king.

Shrek?

Shrek was created by DreamWorks Animation and released in 2001. It was based on a 1990 children’s book by William Steig. The film used groundbreaking computer animation and was originally voiced by Chris Farley, but after his death, Mike Myers took over, giving Shrek his iconic Scottish accent.
